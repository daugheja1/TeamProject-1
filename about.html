About Us

Rick & Todd's Diner was founded during the Great Depression originally, standing as a location that would provide food for relatively cheap prices
while still holding quality. long past the Depression, Rick & Todd's continues its original goal of providing quality meals for affordable prices.
We serve a variety of dishes, not restraining ourselves to any one particular theme or type of food, serving foods ranging from a simple burger to
a full steak dinner.
